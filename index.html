01 — The Problem

Systems in silos.
Officers under pressure.

Every day, over 50,000 cargo shipments pass through Dubai ports and airports. Customs officers have seconds to decide. The X-ray scanner doesn't talk to the declaration database. The database doesn't talk to the risk watchlist. An officer has to manually connect the dots — at scale, every day. That's not a people problem. That's a systems problem.

The Current Reality

Three critical data systems exist — but none of them communicate. Officers must manually cross-reference everything under time pressure and volume stress.

What Smugglers Exploit

Sophisticated networks use misdeclaration, false invoicing, shell entities, and known blind spots in manual inspection to move drugs, weapons, and counterfeit goods through high-volume corridors.

02 — The Solution

SENTRY — a
lie detector for cargo.

We built an AI-powered cargo intelligence system that connects three data streams in real time. Individually, each signal is useful. Together, they're unstoppable.

01

Scanner Images

X-ray and CT scan analysis. Computer vision checks if the physical shape, density, and volume match the declared goods.

02

Declaration Data

HS code validation, weight-to-value anomaly detection, NLP entity analysis, invoice intelligence. Does the paperwork make sense?

03

Risk Intelligence

WCO watchlists, known smuggling route profiling, entity graph connections. Who is sending this — and where from?

03 — How It Works

All three streams.
One decision.

When a shipment arrives, SENTRY runs all three streams simultaneously. One flag is a question. Three flags on the same shipment — that's a hold.

AI

Computer Vision

Analyses the scan and asks: does the shape, density, and volume match what was declared? Detects hidden voids, density anomalies, concealed objects.

DOC

Rules Engine

Checks the declaration: is the weight plausible? Is the invoice value suspicious? Is this a first-time importer with a high-value consignment?

NET

Risk Model

Cross-references watchlists, known narcotics and weapons corridors, and entity graph linkages. Has this consignee appeared in prior flagged shipments?

⚡

Fusion Engine

Cross-references all three in under 2.3 seconds. Produces a 0–100 risk score with specific, actionable flags for the officer.

Every confirmed seizure makes it smarter.

The system learns from every outcome — every seizure, every cleared false positive. Detection improves every single day.
